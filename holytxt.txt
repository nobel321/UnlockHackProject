A PSALM TO LINUX

Oh holy Linux, holy Linux.
Thou art forever my only love.
My heart be filled with joy at your Enter.

Oh holy Linux, holy Linux.
We bring you praise.
We give you all the glory.

Oh holy Linux, holy Linux.
May you forever be,
Control, Alt and Delete free.

- Quintin Beukes (https://allpoetry.com/poems/about/linux)
